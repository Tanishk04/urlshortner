Why Choose Our URL Shortener?

FAQ

Common questions

Here are some of the most common questions that we get.
What is a shortened URL? A shortened URL is a condensed version of a long URL that redirects to the original website.
How does the custom alias feature work? The custom alias feature allows you to create a personalized and memorable alias for your shortened URL.
Can I change the custom alias after creating it? Yes, We provide option to make changes in registered URL mappings.
Is there a limit on the number of URLs I can shorten? No, you can shorten as many URLs as you want using our service.
Are there any restrictions on the types of URLs that can be shortened? We do not allow the shortening of URLs that violate our terms of service, including malicious or inappropriate content.
